Tic Tac Toe

Requirements:-

- Board
- Player
- Computer
- turn

Actions:-

- makeMove()
- checkWinner()
- counter()
- restartGame()



Game Play Logic:-

=> Player
- Check free slots/cells and play.


=> Computer
- Check free slots/cells.
- check if computer can win then play and win.
- check if computer can prevent player form winning then do it.
- play randomly.
